Rental Income Advisor

Who needs the Rental Income Advisor?

Ever thought about being your OWN boss?

Ever thought about owning real estate?

Ever thought you could make some real $$ with a rental property?

Ever wanted to a quick and easy way to "run the numbers" on that hot new property?

If so... this calculator is for YOU!

All you need to do is enter 3 basic pieces of information about your potential investment property:

  1. The value of property under consideration

  2. The amount of monthly mortgage payment

  3. The amount of monthly rent that is estimated to be generated from this property

How does the Rental Income Advisor work?

You can choose to further fine-tune the estimate by altering the presets for the following values:

  1. Percent of time the property will be vacant (no rental income) (default: 5.0%)

  2. Percent of property value that must be paid annually in insurance (default: 1.0%)

  3. Percent of property value that must be paid annually in property tax (default: 1.0%)

  4. Percent of rental income that will go to property management (default: 5.0%)

  5. Percent of rental income that will go to repairs (default: 10.0%)

The advisor will provide you with your monthly net income, monthly total income, and monthly expenses - as well as a colorful bar chart to explain where all the $$ goes. This all AUTOMATICALLY updates as you adjust the input features.
